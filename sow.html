About SOW

With the world in the grips of intersecting food, fuel, financial and environmental crises, it is clear that we need to make a shift in the way we eat and live. At SOW it is our mission to enlighten the urban public on the environmental benefits of growing a food garden in our own homes. Through promotion of micro-home gardening and sustainable local food systems, we seek to empower and help individuals achieve a greater level of food self-reliance while doing our part to help our planet.
