About —

Nat.

Big, Mini World—interative installation

As a natural history museum, the Nat explores the entirety of Earth’s residues. In that sense, the Nat is a frozen microcosm of this planet. By taking an everyday object and blowing it up to a world of its own, the Big, Mini World explores the opposite.

As a permanent installation in the museum, it encourages all the visitors to bring their own sample of their world. It could be anything from leaves to a dustball in their desk drawers. With the help of an assistant, the visitor will receive a specimen inside a glass slide, which will be placed under the Nat Microscope. In a matter of seconds, they will experience a world of a vast smallness.
